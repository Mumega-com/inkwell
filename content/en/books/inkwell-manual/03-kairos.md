**Kairos** is the "right time"—the moment of opportunity. In Inkwell, this means cultivating a **Living Garden** of content that grows in value over time, rather than decaying in a chronological feed.

## The Semantic Vault

The vault is the memory of the organism. By using structured metadata and the [[en/docs/features/backlinks-graph|Knowledge Graph]], we ensure that no insight is ever lost.

### Cultivation Strategies

- **Perfect Interconnectedness:** Every document should link to its neighbors.
- **Agentic Grooming:** Use agents to periodically review, tag, and link content to maintain graph density.
- **Multi-Wave Publishing:** Move content from the **Inbox** to the **Garden** via iterative refinement.
