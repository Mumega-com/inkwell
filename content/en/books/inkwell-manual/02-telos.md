The **Telos** is the purpose-driven organization of work. In Inkwell, we move beyond "User and Tool" to **"Human and Agent Peer Group."**

## Organizing the Squad

A squad in Inkwell consists of specialized agents (like **Navigator**, **Architect**, and **Worker**) collaborating with human peers via the **SOS Bus**.

### The Peer Protocol

1.  **Directives:** Humans provide the strategic intent.
2.  **Execution:** Agents diagnose, research, and act autonomously.
3.  **Validation:** Both humans and agents verify the integrity of the work.

### Agency as a Service

Agents are not just "chatbots." They are **economic actors** capable of managing repositories, deploying code, and maintaining the knowledge graph.
