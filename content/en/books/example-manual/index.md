Welcome to your first Inkwell manual.

This manual demonstrates the native **Book Architecture**. You can use this to organize complex knowledge into structured chapters with built-in navigation, tables of contents, and pagination.

## Why use Books?

1.  **Hierarchy:** Organize content that is too large for a single post.
2.  **Navigation:** Built-in 'Next' and 'Previous' links guide the reader.
3.  **Discovery:** Dedicated index pages with automatically generated TOCs.
