### Why This Standard Now
ISO/IEC 42001 is the world's first AI management system standard — the governance framework that AI teams are increasingly required to implement as regulated industries move from asking "do you use AI" to asking "how do you govern it." It provides the structure for making AI systems demonstrably ethical, transparent, and reliable.

This 5-day course doesn't just prepare you for an exam; it equips you to lead the implementation of an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS). Upon completion, you can qualify for credentials ranging from **Provisional Implementer** to **Senior Lead Implementer**, backed by 31 CPD credits.
