Inkwell is a full business operating system — not just a website builder. This course takes you from a blank config file to a running business: live dashboard, signed contracts, active ad campaigns, and a system that gets smarter every day.

Eight lessons. The first three are free. Lessons 4–8 cover the paid-tier features: dashboard, contracts, campaigns, chat, and the weekly data review.

## Who This Is For

Small business owners, solo operators, and agencies who want to run their business from a single interface — without stitching together five different SaaS tools. You don't need to be a developer. You need to be able to follow instructions and copy a config file.

## What You'll Have at the End

- A branded site running on your domain
- Content published and indexed by Google
- Live data from Google Search Console and Analytics in your dashboard
- A contract portal your customers can sign on their phone
- A Telegram bot you use to steer the business
- A paid ad campaign with conversion tracking
- A weekly review habit that compounds over time

## Lesson Summaries

**Lesson 1 — Configure Your Site (Free)**
Copy the example config, set your name and colors, toggle the features you want, and run the dev server. By the end you have a site running locally with your brand.

**Lesson 2 — Publish Your First Page (Free)**
Learn how content collections work, write a blog post in markdown, and drop it in the right folder. Within seconds it's live.

**Lesson 3 — Connect Your Data Sources (Free)**
Connect Google Search Console and Google Analytics via MCP. By the end, real traffic data flows into your dashboard endpoint.

**Lesson 4 — Set Up Your Dashboard (Paid)**
Walk through all five dashboard views, understand the KPI cards, and set up the daily cron so your data updates automatically every morning.

**Lesson 5 — Launch Your Contract Portal (Paid)**
Create a contract via the API, configure notifications via Twilio and Resend, and send a real contract for signing. Your customer signs on their phone in under two minutes.

**Lesson 6 — Add Chat and Telegram (Paid)**
Enable the chat widget and set up your Telegram bot. You'll be able to check leads, pull status, and respond to customers without opening a browser.

**Lesson 7 — Run Your First Campaign (Paid)**
Connect Google Ads, create a landing page, set up conversion tracking, and launch at $10/day. The ARROW framework keeps you focused on what matters.

**Lesson 8 — Read Your Data and Grow (Paid)**
The weekly review system. What to check every Monday, which KPIs to watch, when to increase budget vs. change strategy.
